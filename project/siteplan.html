Site Plan

Reason for the Name

I chose the name "CoinEd" for my website because the main cryptocurrency website is called Coinbase, so I was thinking of something that would be similar. Since this is an educational website as well, I figured calling it Coin Education but adding them together sounded better as CoinEd.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help people understand the importance of cryptocurrency in an investment portfolio, and at the same time educate them on how to open up an account to start buying and selling cryptocurrency. Also, the 3rd page has a live chart of 3 of the top cryptocurrencies so they can see the price change in real time. Those 3 are Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and Litecoin (LTC).

Scenarios

Scenario #1

Website Visitor: Someone who knows nothing about Cryptocurrency
Question: "What is Crypto and why should I learn about it?"
Content Focus: Explain what cryptocurrency is, why it is important, and what the future for cryptocurrency will entail. Explain it as if the website were for a child so that even the most novice investor can understand the purpose of cryptocurrency.

Scenario #2

Website Visitor: A person who is interested in opening an account to trade cryptocurrency
Question: "How do I buy and sell crypto?"
Content Focus: Step-by-step education for potential crypto investors in the USA on how to open a Coinbase account to be able to deposit their money into the crypto exchange and how to buy and sell crypto on the exchange.
